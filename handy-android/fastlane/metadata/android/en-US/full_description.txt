Handy for Android is an offline, on-device speech-to-text dictation engine. It captures microphone audio, processes it through a local Whisper-family model, and injects the transcribed text into any application — without requiring an internet connection.

Key features:
• Works entirely offline — your voice stays on your device
• On-device AI with Whisper GGUF models (tiny, small, medium, turbo, large)
• Input Method Service (IME) for text injection into any app
• Shizuku-based power-user injection (optional, for advanced users)
• Voice Activity Detection for automatic silence filtering
• Post-processing: filler word removal, stutter collapse
• Model download and management
• Transcription history with search
• Material You dynamic color theming
• Foreground Service for reliable recording

Permissions:
• Microphone — required for speech recognition
• Notification (Android 13+) — required for Foreground Service
• Shizuku (optional) — for advanced text injection

Privacy:
No data leaves your device. All processing is done locally using on-device AI models.
